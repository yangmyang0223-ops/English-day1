🎨 Day 4 – Landscape Painting

✍️ Main Sentence Patterns

  • Start with a light sketch of the horizon.
  • Layer colors from background to foreground.

提示：有底線的英文都能點擊發音。

🖊️ Vocabulary and Phrases

Word/PhraseKKExample中文
horizon/həˈraɪzən/Keep the horizon straight in the composition.地平線要保持水平。
foreground/ˈfɔːrˌɡraʊnd/Add texture to the foreground grass.為前景草地增加質感。
background/ˈbækˌɡraʊnd/Blur the background slightly.把背景稍微模糊。
depth/dɛpθ/Use value shifts to suggest depth.用明暗變化呈現景深。
atmospheric perspective/ˌætməˈsfɛrɪk pərˈspɛktɪv/Cooler colors help the distance recede.冷色讓遠方往後退。
