📝 Quiz

  1. What does 'composition' refer to in art?
    • A. The colors used
    • B. The brush strokes
    • C. The arrangement of elements
    • D. The canvas size
    Answer: C – Composition is how visual elements are arranged in an artwork.
  2. What is a 'focal point'?
    • A. The border of the painting
    • B. The darkest area
    • C. The area that draws attention
    • D. The background
    Answer: C – The focal point is the part of the image that draws the viewer’s eye.
  3. What is the 'rule of thirds'?
    • A. A painting rule about colors
    • B. A guideline dividing the canvas into thirds
    • C. A perspective technique
    • D. A shading method
    Answer: B – The rule of thirds is a compositional guideline dividing the canvas into thirds.
  4. Which technique helps create depth?
    • A. Leading lines
    • B. Flat brush
    • C. Saturation
    • D. Highlighting
    Answer: A – Leading lines guide the viewer’s eye and can help create depth.
  5. What does perspective do?
    • A. Make the image brighter
    • B. Add texture
    • C. Create space and depth
    • D. Focus on color harmony
    Answer: C – Perspective gives the sense of depth and space.
