📝 Quiz

  1. What is a canvas?
    • A. A kind of paint
    • B. A type of brush
    • C. A surface used for painting
    • D. A drawing tool
    Answer: C – A canvas is a surface used for painting.
  2. What is the purpose of a sketch?
    • A. To frame the artwork
    • B. To fill in colors
    • C. To create a rough outline
    • D. To clean the brushes
    Answer: C – A sketch is a rough outline made before painting.
  3. Which item is used to support a canvas?
    • A. Palette
    • B. Brush
    • C. Easel
    • D. Table
    Answer: C – An easel supports the canvas while painting.
  4. What does 'fill in' mean in painting?
    • A. Sketching quickly
    • B. Adding color to shapes
    • C. Washing brushes
    • D. Mixing colors
    Answer: B – 'Fill in' means adding color to shapes.
  5. Why use light strokes?
    • A. To darken the canvas
    • B. To build form gently
    • C. To erase mistakes
    • D. To clean the surface
    Answer: B – Light strokes help build form gradually and softly.
