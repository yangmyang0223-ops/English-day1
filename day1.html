✍️ Main Sentence Patterns

  • I'm starting with a simple sketch. — 我從一張簡單的草圖開始。
  • I use light strokes to shape the form. — 我用輕筆觸建立結構。

🖌️ Vocabulary and Phrases

Word/Phrase KK Phonetic Example Sentence 中文翻譯
canvas /ˈkæn.vəs/ She painted a landscape on a large canvas. 她在一塊大畫布上畫了一幅風景畫。
easel /ˈiː.zəl/ The painting is displayed on an easel. 畫作放在畫架上展示。
sketch /skɛtʃ/ He made a quick sketch before painting. 他在上色前先快速勾勒了一張草圖。
outline /ˈaʊtˌlaɪn/ Draw the outline first. 先畫出輪廓。
draw a line /drɔ ə laɪn/ Use a pencil to draw a line across the page. 用鉛筆在紙上畫一條線。
fill in /fɪl ɪn/ Fill in the shapes with color. 把形狀塗上顏色。
light strokes /laɪt stroʊks/ Start with light strokes to build the form. 從輕筆觸開始，建立畫面結構。

📝 Quiz

  1. What is a canvas?
    Answer: C — A canvas is a surface used for painting.
  2. What is the purpose of a sketch?
    Answer: C — A sketch is a rough outline made before painting.
