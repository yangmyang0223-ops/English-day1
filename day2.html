🖊️ Vocabulary and Phrases

Word/PhraseKKExample中文
brush/brʌʃ/Use a soft brush for smooth blending.用軟毛筆刷讓顏色更柔和。
palette/ˈpælɪt/She mixed warm colors on the palette.她在調色盤上調配暖色。
paint/peɪnt/Add a little water to thin the paint.加一點水把顏料稀釋。
watercolor/ˈwɔtərˌkʌlər/Watercolor dries quickly on this paper.水彩在這張紙上很快乾。
acrylic/əˈkrɪlɪk/Acrylic paint is bright and opaque.壓克力顏料鮮明且不透明。
oil paint/ɔɪl peɪnt/Oil paint takes longer to dry.油畫顏料較慢乾。
palette knife/ˈpælɪt naɪf/Use a palette knife to apply thick paint.用調色刀上厚厚的顏料。
