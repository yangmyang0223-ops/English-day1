📝 Quiz

  1. What is a palette used for?
    • A. Holding brushes
    • B. Drying paint
    • C. Mixing colors
    • D. Washing hands
    Answer: C – A palette is used for mixing colors.
  2. What tool is used to apply color to the canvas?
    • A. Ruler
    • B. Eraser
    • C. Paintbrush
    • D. Marker
    Answer: C – A paintbrush is used to apply color.
  3. Which brush is good for large areas?
    • A. Round brush
    • B. Flat brush
    • C. Detail brush
    • D. Fan brush
    Answer: B – A flat brush is ideal for covering large areas.
  4. What is a paper towel used for in painting?
    • A. Blotting the brush
    • B. Sharpening pencils
    • C. Cleaning hands
    • D. Mixing paint
    Answer: A – A paper towel is used to blot and dry the brush.
  5. What do you do with a water container?
    • A. Store pencils
    • B. Rinse brushes
    • C. Hold paint
    • D. Mix glue
    Answer: B – Water containers are used to rinse brushes.
