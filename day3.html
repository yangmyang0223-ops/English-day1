📝 Quiz

  1. What are the primary colors?
    • A. Red, Green, Blue
    • B. Red, Blue, Yellow
    • C. Blue, Orange, Purple
    • D. Yellow, Green, Purple
    Answer: B – Primary colors are red, blue, and yellow.
  2. Which pair is complementary?
    • A. Blue and Orange
    • B. Red and Green
    • C. Yellow and Purple
    • D. All of the above
    Answer: D – All the options listed are complementary color pairs.
  3. What does saturation refer to?
    • A. The brightness of a color
    • B. The lightness or darkness of a color
    • C. The intensity or purity of a color
    • D. The temperature of a color
    Answer: C – Saturation refers to how intense or pure a color is.
  4. What tool helps you understand color relationships?
    • A. Palette
    • B. Color wheel
    • C. Brush
    • D. Ruler
    Answer: B – A color wheel is used to understand color relationships.
  5. What is 'hue'?
    • A. A type of brush
    • B. A color's temperature
    • C. A pure color
    • D. The way light hits the canvas
    Answer: C – Hue refers to a pure color without shade or tint.
